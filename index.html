Add a new Driver

You can add a new driver to your system. A driver has access to a number of vehicles and can change the status of his shipments.

Deliver Any where

Since the drivers have access to the internet, they can login to their accounts throught the website and edit the status of the shipments from anywhere.

Clear Vision

As it's important to keep track on diveres, shipments, and packages, our system gives the admins a complete picture over the state of all parts of the system through a clear interface.

Keep your vehicles safe

Vehicles are the main asset of our business. This is why we make sure to keep track of every vehicle need, who drivers it, and what shipments are on it at any given time.

Track Shipments

It's very important to know where every package is at any given time. This is why we make sure that you can locate where the packages are, their current state, and who is resposible for them.
